About

I am an Assistant Professor with the Department of Mathematical and Statistical Sciences at CU Denver. I am also a Senior Fellow with Schmidt Science Fellows.

Previously, I was a Schmidt Science Fellow and a President's Postdoctoral Fellow with the H. Milton Stewart School of Industrial and Systems Engineering at the Georgia Institute of Technology (2024-2025). My postdoctoral mentor was Alejandro Toriello. I was also a Postdoctoral Fellow at the Simons Laufer Mathematical Sciences Institute (SLMath, formerly MSRI) (2023).

I earned my PhD in Applied Mathematics from the Center for Applied Mathematics at Cornell University (2023). My PhD advisor was Samitha Samaranayake. Prior to that, I earned my BSc in Civil Engineering and minor in Computer Science from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign (2017). My undergraduate research advisor was Daniel B. Work.

I have also participated in semester-long research visits at ICERM and IPAM, research internships at Amazon.com and Bosch North America, and REU mentoring at MSRI and ICERM.

Research

I am broadly interested in both applied and theoretical topics in operations research and algebraic combinatorics. Some specific themes include:
  • Mathematical optimization
  • Game theory
  • Transportation and logistics, and particularly public transit
  • Combinatorics of objects related to permutations, and particularly "parking functions"

Selected Publications

  • Cooperation and the Design of Public Goods, Proceedings of the 26th ACM Conference on Economics and Computation (as extended abstract), 2025. Joint with Alejandro Toriello.
  • Parking functions, Fubini rankings, and Boolean intervals in the weak order of S_n, Journal of Combinatorics, 2025. Joint with Jennifer Elder, Pamela E. Harris, and Jan Kretschmann.
  • Lucky Cars and the Quicksort Algorithm, The American Mathematical Monthly, 2024. Joint with Pamela E. Harris and Jan Kretschmann.
  • On the Value of Dynamism in Transit Networks, Transportation Science, 2023. Joint with M. Grazia Speranza and Samitha Samaranayake.
