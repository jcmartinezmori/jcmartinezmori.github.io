About

I am an Assistant Professor with the Department of Mathematical and Statistical Sciences at CU Denver. Prior to joining CU Denver, I was a Schmidt Science Fellow and a President's Postdoctoral Fellow with the H. Milton Stewart School of Industrial and Systems Engineering at the Georgia Institute of Technology. My postdoctoral mentor is Alejandro Toriello.
